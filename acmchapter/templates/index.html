VASC

The Vardhaman College of Engineering (VCE) Hyderabad Student Chapter
of the ACM was established in 2009 . Goal of the chapter is to create
interest among the students on latest technologies apart from what
they learn during the course work. The chapter organizes talks and
workshops on different technologies by speakers who are well known
in their area. These talks give students opportunities to learn about
advanced research subjects. Apart from these, some technical activities
are also organized.

VASC

ACM, the world’s largest educational and scientific computing society,
delivers resources that advance computing as a science and a profession.
ACM provides the computing field’s premier Digital Library and serves
its members and the computing profession with leading-edge publications,
conferences, and career resources.

  • CareerNews, ACM's twice monthly email career news digest
